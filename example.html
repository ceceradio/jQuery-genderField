Here's a simple gender field, straight out of the box:

$("#gender").genderField();

Let's override the genders included:

$("#gender2").genderField({genders: ['Male', 'Female', 'Nonbinary', 'None']});

We can also remove the "prefer not to disclose" option:

$("#gender3").genderField({genders: ['Male', 'Female', 'Nonbinary', 'None'], allowPreferNotToDisclose: false});
